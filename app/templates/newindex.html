News Bias Detection System

The News Bias Detection System is a software tool designed to detect political bias in news articles.

Use tool

What is Media Bias?

  • The news reporter or news source exhibits inclination towards one particular type of ideology.
  • These are usually categorised as left, left-center, center, right-center, right.
  • For more information click here.
  • We also give results such as article summary, news source wise analysis, author details, and other metadata.
  • We also provide subjectivity and polarity predictions.
  • Subjectivity between 0 to 1, where 0 indicates maximum objectivity and 1 indicates maximum opinionated content.
  • Polarity between -1 and 1 indicates the sentiment, where -1 is negative and +1 is positive.

This web app allows you to get the above results for an article of your choice, or to choose from the news sources we provide.
